Tafros VPN - Quick Start Guide

1. Choosing a device

You can use Tafros VPN on desktop and laptop computers, tablets, and cell phones. Setup on a cell phone or tablet will be fastest since you can use your device's camera to scan the QR code that you will generate following the instructions below. Setup on a dektop or laptop computer will require you to copy and paste some information into a file and save it onto your computer.

2. Install the WireGuard software onto your device

Tafros VPN uses a technology called WireGuard that utilizes state-of-the-art cryptography and was created to be faster, simpler, and leaner than other VPN technologies. To use Tafros VPN, you must have a WireGuard application on your device that acts as a bridge between your device and our secure VPN servers. You can read more about WirgeGuard on the WireGuard website.

3.1 Configuring a connection to Tafros VPN

From a different device than the one that you installed the WireGuard application on, navigate to https://www.tafrosvpn.com.

3.2 Log into your Tafros VPN account

You have been provided with a unique Tafros VPN acount number. Keep that account number somewhere safe so that you can log into the Tafros VPN website in the future and make changes to your account. It is a long string of letters and numbers so it helps to copy and paste it to avoid making mistakes in typing it.

Enter your Account ID code and click SIGN IN:

3.3 Manage your devices

You can configure up to five devices to connect to the Tafros VPN servers. If you do not have any devices configured, your screen will look like this:

3.4 Add a new device

Click ADD DEVICE and select a server from the top menu.

Click the GENERATE KEYPAIR button and you will see a new private key and public key generated for you. WireGuard uses these keys to establish a secure connection between your device and the Tafros VPN servers. You do not need to copy your keys or remember them at all. The Tafros VPN system will manage your keys for you. DO NOT GIVE YOUR PRIVATE KEY TO ANYONE. If someone else were to gain access to your private key, it may be possible for them to compromise the security of your connection to the Tafros VPN servers.

Click the ADD DEVICE button. A unique configuration will be created and displayed to you. It will have a QR code on top (used to connect a cell phone) and a text description of the configuration at the bottom (used to connect a laptop or desktop computer):

4.1 Connect a cell phone or tablet

Open the WireGuard app on your cell phone or tablet and click the plus icon + to Create a new configuration. Select Create from QR code and point your phone at the QR code generated above. The app will prompt you to name the connection. Finally, click the new configuration to connect to the Tafros VPN server. Back in your browser, click the CLOSE button under the QR code.

4.2 Connect a desktop or laptop computer (MacOS and Windows)

If you are using Windows, open Notepad. If you are using MacOS, use a built-in text editor. Select all of the text under the QR code, copy it, and paste it into the application that you just opened. Save the file, giving it a name ending with ".conf". Open the WireGuard application on your computer and click Add Tunnel. Select the new ".conf" file that you just created and click Open. To connect to the Tafros VPN server, select the new tunnel and click the Activate button. Back in your browser, click the CLOSE button under the QR code.

4.3 Verify your connection

To verify that you are connected to the Tafros VPN servers, open a web browser on your device and go to This website. If you are connected to the Tafros VPN servers, it should indicate that your location is the same as the server you selected in step 3.4 above.

Please note that you may need to be more specific when searching for local businesses to include your city/state since your IP is no longer tied to your current location.

5 End notes

  • For now, you cannot change the Tafros VPN server that is tied to a device configuration. To change your server, you need to go through the Add a new device process starting at 3.4 above. You may set up multiple configurations in the WireGuard app on a single physical device or you can delete the old device from the Tafros VPN account management screen and recreate it using a different server.

  • Keep your Account ID safe. Tafros does not have a way to retrieve it for you if you lose it.
